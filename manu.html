Lumbini Tea Factory

Lumbini Tea Factory is an award winning company that is located amid the beautiful sinharaja rainforest in Deniyaya, Southern province. The factory expands within a 100 acre land that provides the lush tea leaves to the factory. The comapany makes different kinds of tea ranging from black tea, white tea and breakfast tea. The company employees arount 150 works. Lumbini Tea Factory also sells their tea in the local market and tourist destinations under the "Lumbini Tea brand".

Uruwala Tea Factory

Uruwala Tea factory, located in Akuress, Southern province is one of the most award winning tea factories in Srilanka. Spaned across 150 acres Uruwala tea facotry is one of the oldest tea factories that is still working in Srilanka. Factory mainly makes pure southern black tea (BOPF) that is mainly being exported to the middle eastern market.

The factory employees around 200 workers across the eastate and the factory. Uruwala tea factory dosent focus on the local market as the majority of the produced tea is being exported to middle eatern and european countries. The comapny is also the only tea factory in Srilanka which is being managed by the same family under the third generation.

Dilmah Tea

Dilmah tea is one of the most popular tea brands in the world. Dilmah tea brand was initiated in 1988. Dilmah is the key private tea exporting brand in Srilanka which is mainly popular in Europe, Middle east, Australia and China. Dilmah also used to be one of the sponsor brands of Srilanka Cricket.
